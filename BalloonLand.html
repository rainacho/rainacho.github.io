Balloon Land

VR / A-FRAME / JavaScript

The VR experience allows a user to interact with balloons and change their environment. The user can look around in the environment and interact with the balloons by staring at it so the cursor that is displayed as a circle in the center of the object, and the balloon will fly up into the air. Users can also use voice commands to change the environment.

To get started, the user loads the website on their phone in landscape mode and clicks the cardboard button in the bottom right corner to enable VR mode. They insert their phone into the VR headset adjust it to their vision.

Users can say    SHOW MENU    HIDE MENU  to bring up or down the Menu



Also Users can say
  GO TO EGYPT,    GO TO FOREST,    GO TO TRON
to show any of the 3 places
